Platform overview

Everything your front desk needs, in one place

Solv connects patients to your clinic online, streamlines check-in, and gives your staff real-time visibility into the waiting room.

📅

Online Scheduling

Patients book same-day or future appointments directly from your listing. No phone tag. No empty slots. Your calendar fills itself.

📋

Digital Check-In

Patients complete registration and intake forms on their own device before they arrive, so your staff spends less time at the front desk and more time with patients.

⏱

Wait Time Transparency

Display real-time estimated wait times online and in automated texts. Patients show up when they're ready — not all at once.

📱

Two-Way Patient Messaging

Send automated appointment reminders, follow-up messages, and review requests. Communicate without phone calls.

📈

Operator Dashboard

Monitor visit volume, wait times, and patient satisfaction scores across every location from a single real-time dashboard.

⭐

Reputation Management

Automatically prompt satisfied patients to leave reviews and surface feedback that helps your clinic improve and stand out online.

Why it matters

Your staff is stretched. Solv gives them time back.

Front desk teams at urgent care centers handle more touchpoints than almost any other medical setting — often with minimal support. Solv automates the repetitive work so your team can focus on patients.

Fewer phone calls, less chaos

Online booking and digital check-in dramatically cut inbound call volume, giving front desk staff breathing room during peak hours.

Predictable patient flow

Wait time visibility and appointment staggering prevent lobby congestion, improving both the patient experience and staff workload.

More revenue, fewer gaps

Automated reminders reduce no-shows, and online discoverability drives new patients to your clinic when nearby competitors are full.

Consistent across every location

Multi-location operators get unified data and consistent workflows across every site — no more one-off processes or siloed reporting.

Patients who come back

A smooth, transparent experience earns loyalty. Patients who booked online and skipped the lobby wait give higher satisfaction scores and return for future visits.
