What it solves

Six problems operators deal with every day

Solv isn't a feature list. It's a direct answer to the operational drag that keeps you reactive instead of strategic.

01
"Our phones never stop ringing."

Online booking and AI scheduling let patients book, check in, and get wait time updates without ever calling. Most operators see inbound call volume drop by half within weeks.

02
"No-shows are killing our revenue."

Automated reminders go out at the right intervals — by text, not just email. Patients confirm or reschedule in one tap. That slot goes to someone else, not to waste.

03
"I'm flying blind until end of day."

A real-time dashboard shows live visit volume, wait times, and satisfaction across every site. Know what's happening at Site 3 without calling Site 3.

04
"Every location does things differently."

Solv standardizes patient intake, check-in flows, and messaging across all your sites. New staff learn one workflow — the right one, regardless of location.

05
"Patients leave when they can't see a wait time."

Live wait times display online and update automatically. Patients who can see the wait show up. Patients who can't go to your competitor.

06
"We keep getting negative Google reviews."

Solv prompts satisfied patients to leave reviews right after their visit. More 5-stars, and early warnings about experience issues before they go public.
